## Doc: Next Milestone for Mirror Loop

### Recommendation
The next milestone for Mirror Loop should be the implementation of a more complex axis recognition system that includes emotional states alongside physical movements. This means expanding the current left-right axis to include an emotional spectrum such as calm-agitated. This addition will make Mirror Loop feel fundamentally different from a normal narrative game by introducing a layer of psychological depth that reacts to the player's choices and actions.

### What we give up
By focusing on emotional state recognition, we delay the development of other potential axes such as social interactions or environmental manipulation. If we observe that players are not engaging deeply with the emotional layer or if the technical challenges prove too great, we might need to pivot back to simpler axes or consider integrating social dynamics sooner.

### Signals
- **1-week checkpoint:** Successful integration of the first emotional state into the game mechanics, evidenced by at least three distinct emotional states being recognized and reflected in the game's narrative and visuals.
- **1-month checkpoint:** Player feedback indicating a strong sense of personal investment due to the game's ability to recognize and respond to their emotional states, as measured by survey results and qualitative feedback from playtesting sessions.
