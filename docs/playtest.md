# Playtest

How we gather real-user feedback before cutting a release.

## Who playtests
Three to five trusted friends, ideally including one who hasn't seen
the project yet. Diversity of context matters more than count.

## What we ask
- 'Show me where it stopped being interesting.'
- 'What did you think it was going to do that it didn't?'
- 'What did you find surprising — good or bad?'

## What we don't ask
- Numerical ratings.
- 'Did you like it?' (the answer is always yes).

## After
Notes get filed in docs/playtest_notes/<date>-<initials>.md. Decisions
end up in docs/decisions.md if they alter direction.
